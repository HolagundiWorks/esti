**Top cross-cutting issues**

1. **Two marketing visual systems** — `lp2-ds` (flat, hairlines) vs `esti-blog` (frosted glass cards on gradient). Hurts brand continuity.
2. **Nomenclature drift** in long-tail SEO pages and older blog posts (“practice OS”, “project management software”).
3. **Navigation** — Blog/About/Wiki don’t use `PLATFORM_LANDING_NAV`; rail links differ per route.
4. **CTA clarity** — Demo/signup flows should always label **AORMS-Studio** vs platform.
