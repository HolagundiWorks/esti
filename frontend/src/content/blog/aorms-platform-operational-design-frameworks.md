Most ?practice software? still assumes you are **delivering the client?s project** ?
Gantt charts, task boards, burndown, resource levelling. That is the wrong model for
**consulting offices that advise**.

AORMS is built for **AEC consulting firms** ? architecture and engineering practices
that **advise** on built-environment projects. We are not in solution delivery. We are
not a construction project-management vendor.

## Two frameworks on one spine

Every advisory consultancy needs two things at once:

**Operational framework** ? how the office runs: intake, process standards, review
chains, audit trails, governed knowledge, and the tools that replace scattered
messaging, advisory workflows, documentation, email, sheets, and file sharing.

**Design framework** ? how engagements are structured: methodologies, deliverable
models, templates, and versioned advisory patterns ? not a generic task board for
the client?s build programme.

AORMS ships both on one operational spine.

## EOMS vs ESTI

**EOMS** (Emergent Object Management System) is the **knowledge bank** ? a
continuously-learning catalog of standard codebooks and building/compliance codes.
AORMS apps query EOMS over its API for authoritative data before anything unvalidated
enters the firm library.

**ESTI** (Embedded Studio Intelligence) is the **internal AI agent** ? it answers
only from **validated firm repositories** inside **AORMS-Studio** (Ask ESTI, Studio
Intelligence, ESTI Pulse).

## What ships today

The live architecture app is **AORMS-Studio** ? for Indian architecture and
design **consultancies** advising clients on fees, revisions, GST, drawings, and
studio operations. It is not construction project management. Workspaces are
**web-only** (browser); there is no desktop shell.

**AORMS-Consultancy** for engineering consultancies shares the same platform spine
and is code-complete ? public launch is gated on human walkthrough sign-off.
