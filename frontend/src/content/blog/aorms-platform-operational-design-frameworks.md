Most “practice software” still assumes you are **delivering the client’s project** —
Gantt charts, task boards, burndown, resource levelling. That is the wrong model for
**consulting offices that advise**.

AORMS is built for firms that **consult** in risk management, education, auditing,
and AEC. We are not in solution delivery. We are not a client project-management
vendor.

## Two frameworks on one spine

Every advisory consultancy needs two things at once:

**Operational framework** — how the office runs: intake, process standards, review
chains, audit trails, governed knowledge, and the tools that replace scattered
messaging, advisory workflows, documentation, email, sheets, and file sharing.

**Design framework** — how engagements are structured: methodologies, deliverable
models, templates, and versioned advisory patterns — not a generic task board for
the client’s build programme.

AORMS ships both on one operational spine.

## EmOI vs ESTI

**EmOI** (Embedded Operational Intelligence) is the platform intelligence layer —
dual-tier AI (external validation + internal RAG), workflow intelligence, and
governed answers across verticals.

**ESTI** (Embedded Studio Intelligence) is **architecture-only** — the intelligence
layer inside **AORMS-Studio** (Ask ESTI, Studio Intelligence, ESTI Pulse). Other
vertical workspaces use EmOI in product language.

## What ships today

The first live workspace is **AORMS-Studio** — for Indian architecture and
design **consultancies** advising clients on fees, revisions, GST, drawings, and
studio operations. It is not construction project management.

Risk, education, and auditing workspaces are on the roadmap — same platform primitives,
domain-specific frameworks.
